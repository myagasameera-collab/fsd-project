AI Engineer Career Roadmap

Step 1

Learn Programming Basics (Python recommended)

Step 2

Study Data Structures and Algorithms

Step 3

Learn Mathematics for AI (Linear Algebra, Probability, Statistics)

Step 4

Learn Machine Learning Concepts and Algorithms

Step 5

Study Deep Learning using frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch

Step 6

Work on AI Projects (Chatbots, Prediction Models, Image Recognition)

Step 7

Build a strong portfolio and apply for AI/ML Engineer roles
